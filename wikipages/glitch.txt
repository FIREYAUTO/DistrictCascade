The Glitch stat is a purple bar that is half the height of your XP bar, and is also located inside of your XP bar.

Glitch is only obtainable when the {{seasons:season}} is halloween.

To increase your glitch stat, input the incorrect answer on a {{generators:claimable generator}}.

When your glitch stat is maxed out, you will recieve the following if you don't already have them:
^ Glitch Dasher Skin
^ {{orbs:Glitch Orb}}
