Canned Drinks are obtained by purchasing them from {{vendingmachines:Vending Machines}}.

The effects of canned drinks are ended upon starting a time trial.

## List of Canned Drinks
^ Canned District Cola
| Using this canned drink will increase your WalkSpeed from `26` to `34` for 30 seconds. Dying does not remove the effect.
