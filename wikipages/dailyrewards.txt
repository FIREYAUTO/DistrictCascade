Daily rewards are given to group members. Every day when you log in, you will get a daily reward.

# Set Rewards
Set rewards are daily rewards you get at specific days
```
None
```

# Repetitive Rewards
Repetitive rewards are daily rewards you get every # amount of days
```
Every 1 Day: Currency
	Calculated as: clamp(floor((Days-1)*5)+10,10,50)
Every 2 Days (Even): +1x : 15m XP Booster (1)
Every 2 Days (Odd): Epic Skin Crate (1)
Every 4 Days: Exotic Skin Crate (1)
```
